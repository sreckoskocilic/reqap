Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how reqap ("we", "the Service") handles information when you use the Service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to operate the Service:

  • Uploaded files. EPUB and TXT files you upload are processed in memory to generate the requested output. They are not saved to persistent storage by reqap.
  • Request parameters. Options you select (report type, LLM mode, chapter selection) are used solely to fulfill your request.
  • Server logs. Standard web server logs (IP address, timestamp, HTTP method, response status) may be retained for operational purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your uploaded content is used exclusively to generate the report you requested. We do not use your content for training, analytics, or any other purpose.

3. Third-Party LLM Providers

Depending on the LLM mode you select, your content is sent to one or more third-party providers:

  • Anthropic (Claude models) — Privacy Policy
  • Google (Gemini models) — Privacy Policy
  • Groq (Llama models) — Privacy Policy
  • Ollama (offline mode) — runs entirely on your local machine; no data is sent externally.

We are not responsible for how these providers handle data. Please review their policies before uploading sensitive content.

4. Data Retention

reqap does not persistently store uploaded files or generated reports. Once your request is complete, the content is discarded. Server logs may be retained for up to 30 days for operational and security purposes.

5. Cookies and Tracking

The Service does not use cookies, analytics, or any tracking technologies.

6. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect data in transit. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will indicate the date of the most recent revision at the top of this page. Continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on the project repository.
